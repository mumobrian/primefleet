About Fredrick Mwaluko

Fleet & Tyre Engineering Specialist

Fredrick Mwaluko is a seasoned Fleet and Tyre Engineering Specialist with over 17 years of experience across transport, logistics, and industrial operations.

He currently serves as a Technical Manager at Mandela Group of Companies, where he leads fleet performance optimization, tyre lifecycle management, and safety-driven engineering initiatives for commercial and industrial clients.

In addition, he is the Founder & CEO of PrimeFleet Solutions, an independent consulting firm focused on helping organizations reduce operational costs, improve safety, and maximize asset performance through structured, data-driven strategies.

His dual role uniquely positions him at the intersection of real-world operations and high-level consulting, enabling him to deliver practical, results-oriented solutions.

Case Studies

Fleet Cost Optimization – Logistics Sector

Challenge: A major logistics operator faced 22% above-budget tyre spend, chronic sidewall failures, and 14% monthly fleet downtime due to poor alignment and inconsistent inflation practices.

Solution: Executed deep-dive axle alignment audits, launched a digital CPK tracking dashboard, initiated a retread pilot program for 200 casings, and delivered driver workshops on inflation discipline.

31% Cost Reduction18% Availability Increase
✓ ROI achieved in 8 months

Safety Improvement Initiative

Challenge: Cross-border transport fleet experienced high blowout incidents with 35% year-over-year increase in safety-related events, impacting insurance premiums and driver confidence.

Solution: Comprehensive failure forensics analysis, implementation of pressure monitoring protocols across all depots, and preventive maintenance scheduling integrated with telematics data.

76% Fewer Incidents12% Fuel Savings
✓ Safety rating improved

Lifecycle Extension – Heavy Duty

Challenge: Industrial mining fleet suffered severe casing damage from harsh terrain conditions, with 28% of tyre budget lost to premature scrapping and frequent replacement cycles.

Solution: Deployed tyre lifecycle management system, integrated Vipal retread processes, conducted monthly engineering audits, and optimized tyre application for terrain conditions.

22% Longer Lifespan28% Fewer Delays
✓ $500K+ annual savings
